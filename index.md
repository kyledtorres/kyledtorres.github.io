### Effect of ECMO Duration on Post-Transplant Survival

ECMO (Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation) is a medical device that provides life support for patients in need of a heart-transplant. I collaborated with mentor Dr. Eric Kawaguchi and my peers to examine whether the duration of being on ECMO is associated with worse health outcomes prior to transplantation. In our research, we implemented various statistical methods such as survival analysis and multinomial regression to explore the relationship between duration on device and post-transplant outcomes.
