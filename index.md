### TidyTuesday
TidyTuesday is a weekly data project organized by the R4DS Online Learning Community. Each week a new dataset is posted, giving users the opportunity to explore data from a variety of different disciplines. In this project, I practice tidying data as well as creating visualizations that effectively communicate relationships found in the data.

### Effect of ECMO Duration on Post-Transplant Survival

ECMO is a medical device that provides life support for patients in need of a heart-transplant. I collaborated with mentor Dr. Eric Kawaguchi and my peers to examine whether the duration of being on ECMO was associated with worse health outcomes post-transplantation. In our research, we implemented various statistical models such as survival analysis and multinomial regression.
